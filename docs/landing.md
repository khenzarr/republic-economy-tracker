## FAQ

### Is this official?
Republic Economy Tracker is a community-driven, open-source project. While not officially affiliated with the Republic core team, we provide transparent, on-chain data directly from the blockchain.

### How are metrics calculated?
All metrics are calculated automatically from on-chain data via direct RPC connection. Our scoring algorithm is open-source and auditable.

### Can I submit my project?
Yes! Connect your Keplr wallet and submit your project through our submission form. All submissions are reviewed by admins before approval.

### Is the code open-source?
Absolutely. The entire codebase is MIT licensed and available on GitHub. Fork it, audit it, or contribute to it.

### How often is data updated?
Chain statistics update every 30 seconds. Project metrics are recalculated hourly via automated cron jobs.

### Can I run my own instance?
Yes! Check our deployment guide for instructions on running your own instance of the tracker.
